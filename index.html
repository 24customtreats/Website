Getting to Know Ayesha - Our Chief Tastemaker

Ayesha was captivated when she first came across custom-designed cookies in 2017 while visiting America for her nephew's 1st birthday. The urge to give her children a similar experience was the start of her incredible journey to becoming a cookie artist. What started as a creative pursuit to create something special for family and friends became a passion and a full-time business known as 24 Custom Treats.
Growing 24 Custom Treats has allowed Ayesha to express her creativity while learning new skills and techniques as she decorates the ever-changing edible canvas made of cookie dough while bringing a smile to the face of her customers. Being able to part-take in her clients' special moments - making them memorable and sweeter - is the core driving force for Ayesha.
